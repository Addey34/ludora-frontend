Privacy Policy

Home

Ludora is a personal, non-commercial project. This page explains what data is collected when you use the site.

Authentication data (localStorage)

Three keys are stored in your browser's localStorage to maintain your session between page loads:

  • ludora-nakama-device-id — a random UUID that identifies your anonymous account.
  • ludora-nakama-session and ludora-nakama-refresh — session tokens that keep you connected.

Clearing your browser storage removes these keys and disconnects you from your account.

Scores & progress

When you are signed in, your scores are recorded on our server (Oracle Cloud, Marseille, France) under your display name: on each game's own online leaderboard and on the cross-game Ludora Points ranking. These leaderboards are publicly visible to other players, who see your display name and score. Level progress is stored on the server too and cached locally as a UI preference only.

Guests can play without publishing anything. If a guest chooses "Sign in to save", the run is stashed in localStorage only long enough to complete sign-in, then submitted to the server. Offline scores are not queued.

Google Sign-In (optional)

If you sign in with Google, Google Identity Services sends us a token containing your display name, email address, and Google ID. We use this solely to link your anonymous account to your Google account so your scores carry over. Your email is not stored for marketing and is not shared with third parties.

Your Google display name is saved on your account as your public name and is the name shown next to your scores on the leaderboards. You can change it at any time on your profile page; the name you choose is kept and is not overwritten on later sign-ins.

Third-party services

  • Google Identity Services — optional sign-in button. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — icon font loaded from Cloudflare's CDN on every page.

Hosting

  • Backend (scores & multiplayer): Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, Marseille, France.

No tracking

No analytics scripts, advertising cookies, or IP address logging beyond what hosting providers handle by default.
