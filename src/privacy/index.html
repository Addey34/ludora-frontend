Privacy Policy

Home

Games Zone is a personal, non-commercial project. This page explains what data is collected when you use the site.

Authentication data (localStorage)

Three keys are stored in your browser's localStorage to maintain your session between page loads:

  • gz-nakama-device-id — a random UUID that identifies your anonymous account.
  • gz-nakama-session and gz-nakama-refresh — session tokens that keep you connected.

Clearing your browser storage removes these keys and disconnects you from your account.

Scores & progress (server-side only)

For games with an online leaderboard (Snake, 2048, Tetris, Typing), your scores are stored only on our server (Oracle Cloud, Marseille, France) — nothing is written to localStorage. Level progress (Pac-Man) is stored on the server and cached locally as a UI preference only.

For local-only games (Breakout, Pong, Memory, Ludo, Connect 4, Battleship, Game of the Goose), scores remain in your browser's localStorage and are never sent to any server.

Google Sign-In (optional)

If you sign in with Google, Google Identity Services sends us a token containing your display name, email address, and Google ID. We use this solely to link your anonymous account to your Google account so your scores carry over. Your email is not stored for marketing and is not shared with third parties.

Third-party services

  • Google Identity Services — optional sign-in button. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — icon font loaded from Cloudflare's CDN on every page.

Hosting

  • Frontend: Render Inc., 525 Brannan St Suite 300, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA.
  • Backend (scores & multiplayer): Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, Marseille, France.

No tracking

No analytics scripts, advertising cookies, or IP address logging beyond what hosting providers handle by default.
